Bio-Informatica tool Noxia Pro

This site will search if a certain substance is noxia (dangerous in latin) for a certain species. This will be displayed in a graph. It will get its information from pubmed.

With graph-check you can check the relationship between a substance and three model organisms. These organisms are: Danio rerio, Dictyostelium discoideum and Caenorhabditis elegans.

The information which the graph will provide is from articles. These articles are available under the graph in a table. The articles are from pubMed and are in English. If the wanted substance is not available the option toxic-substances will help you further. This option adds the wanted substance and a matching organism.
